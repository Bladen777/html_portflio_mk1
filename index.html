Braden Kowalchuk

Born and raised around Hafford, SK, I grew up on an acreage where I had the opportunity to experiment, practice, and develop skills in mechanics and construction. As a graduate of Medicine Hat College with a degree in Applied Arts, I found ways to combine my interest in art and design with skills I've learned from growing up in my rural community, studying computer-aided design and manufacturing, and training as an electrician.

As a craftsman with a special interest in technology, mechanics, construction, and arts and heritage, I've worked with a variety of organizations including Studio Y Creations, Tweak Designs, Medalta in the Historic Clay District, Pad Printer, Alliance Energy, Saskatoon Scotiabank Theatre, and Saskatoon Fire and Flood.

While living in Medicine Hat (home of Canada's preeminent international ceramics residency and center) I found my niche in sculpture. I love to employ mechanical and natural elements in my work.
